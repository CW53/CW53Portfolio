Who am I?

I am a data scientist with a passion for teasing out visual stories from a dataset and questioning results after every finding. I am trying to be a different kind of data scientist because my ultimate goal is to become a geopolitical data scientist who connects regional expertise with the power of data

Most of my time is spent staring at a computer and questioning everything. Outside of work, I like to volunteer at my local animal shelter by working with dogs, in addition, I appreciate what a good sweat can do for the mind and body. I have an eclectic mix of interests, but the top themes can be boiled down to: real estate, animal welfare, the stock market, and ecommerce.

My skills include data analysis, data visualization, and machine learning, particularly as it pertains to predictive analytics. I know you'll see I listed a lot of things, rest assure I do know my stuff, but be aware that as I acquire more knowledge I am likely to make a mistake or mis-recall because I am building a pyramid and sometimes a stepping stone may get chipped (a mistake) as I make my way to the top.
