Who am I?

I am a data scientist with a passion for teasing out visual stories from a dataset and questioning results after every finding. I am trying to be a different kind of data scientist because my ultimate goal is to become a geopolitical analysst who connects regional expertise with the power of data

Most of my time is spent staring at a computer and questioning everything. Outside of work, I like to volunteer at my local animal shelter by working with dogs, in addition, I appreciate what a good sweat can do for the mind and body. I have an eclectic mix of interests, but the top themes can be boiled down to: real estate, animal welfare, the stock market, and ecommerce.

My skills include data analysis, data visualization, and machine learning, particularly as it pertains to time series forecasting/predictive analytics.
